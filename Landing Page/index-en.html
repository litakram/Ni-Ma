Our Services
Solar Powered IoT Device

We analyze environmental data through Solar powered IoT sensors to detect threats such as fires or illegal activities.

AI-Powered Alerts

We use Ai to analyze sounds and sensors data and send alerts to patrol teams with strategies for forest protection.

Custom App and Direct Interaction

We provide a custom app and website with an easy-to-use interface for field teams to interact with the system and manage alerts.



About Us

Our Solution is An innovative solution for environmental monitoring and conservation, presented by the Forest Rangers team at the GAIAthon '24 hackathon.

We are a creative team of five individuals with a shared passion for the environment, artificial intelligence, and innovation. We believe we can make a difference and protect forests from threats.

Our project aims to provide integrated solutions for forest protection against environmental risks and improve the time response of patrol teams through AI technologies.

Ni’ma aims to tackle the challenges of deforestation by providing real-time monitoring, enabling rapid and effective intervention, thus reducing damage and risks.
